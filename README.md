## Task 2: SEO Audit & Keyword Research

**Website Selected:** Brew Haven Cafe  
**Business Type:** Local Cafe

### SEO Audit Observations

- Some pages were loading slowly due to large images.
- Some pages lacked proper meta descriptions, affecting visibility in Google search results.
- Website headings were not properly arranged — a few pages had more than one H1 heading.
- The website was not fully mobile-friendly; some buttons and text appeared too small on mobile.
- A few page titles were very short and did not contain important keywords.

### Target Keywords

| # | Keyword                  |
|---|--------------------------|
| 1 | best cafe near me        |
| 2 | coffee shop in Goa       |
| 3 | cold coffee near me      |
| 4 | cafe with free wifi      |
| 5 | best desserts in Goa     |
| 6 | work friendly cafe       |
| 7 | local coffee cafe        |
| 8 | weekend cafe offers      |
| 9 | cute cafes in Goa        |
| 10 | best snacks and coffee  |

### Improvement Suggestions

- Compress images to improve website loading speed.
- Add proper meta titles and descriptions on every page.
- Use only one H1 heading per page.
- Improve the mobile version of the website.
- Use local keywords to attract nearby customers.

### What I Learned

From this task, I learned how SEO helps websites rank better on Google. I also understood how keywords, page speed, and mobile optimization are important for improving website traffic.

### Conclusion

This was a good learning experience — I gained practical knowledge about SEO auditing and keyword research, and understood how even small improvements can help a business website perform better online.

## Task 5: Web Analytics & Reporting

**Scenario:** The website receives around 10,000 visitors every month, but the bounce rate is very high (85%) and conversion rate is very low (0.5%). Most traffic comes from social media.

### Possible Reasons for High Bounce Rate

- Website landing page may not match the social media ads.
- Slow loading speed may cause users to leave quickly.
- Website design may not be mobile-friendly.
- Users may not clearly understand what action to take.

### Suggestions to Improve Conversion Rate

- Improve landing page design and make CTA buttons clearer.
- Reduce page loading time by compressing images.
- Create targeted offers and discounts for social media visitors.

### KPIs to Track

| KPI                         | Description                                      |
|-----------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Bounce Rate                 | Percentage of visitors who leave without action  |
| Conversion Rate             | Percentage of visitors who complete a goal       |
| Average Session Duration    | How long users stay on the site                  |
| Click-Through Rate (CTR)    | Ratio of clicks to impressions                   |
| Traffic Source Performance  | Which channels drive the most valuable traffic   |

### Conclusion

These internship tasks helped in understanding the basics of digital marketing, including social media planning, website performance analysis, and SEO (Search Engine Optimization).
